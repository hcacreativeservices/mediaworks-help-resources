Simplified Ordering with Automated Billing

A new billing process will be implemented this October that will speed up ordering through MediaWorks. Instead of manually entering department billing info at check out, each MediaWorks order will be automatically billed to the COID & Dept# associated with the user's HCA information. Ordering will be easier for users and will ensure orders are not held up due to billing information errors.

MediaWorks Support moving to a new portal is coming soon.

MediaWorks Support (currently zendesk) will be moving to a new portal this November. More Information will be available soon.

Search Within A Category

Users can now choose to either search within the current category or the entire site. Click "Category" on the toggle switch in the search bar to search within the current category you are in rather than the entire site.
